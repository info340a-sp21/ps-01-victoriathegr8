All About Amaya

Amaya is a character in the Netflix series The Dragon Prince. She is the aunt of two of the main characters, Callum and Ezran. She is also the general and leader of the Standing Battallion, an army that protects the The Border of Katolis, a kingdom in the series. She is notable, as she is deaf and uses sign language to communicate.

There's many reasons to admire Amaya, but here's just a few:

  • She is extremely caring, and acts as a mother figure to her nephews after they lose their mother.
  • She was able to befriend someone who was previously her enemy, Janai, a Sunfire Elf.
  • She never portrays being deaf and using sign language as a detriment.
  • She was able to face the light of the Sunforge safely, with showed she had a pure heart.
